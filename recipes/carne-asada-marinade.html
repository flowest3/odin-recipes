Carne Asada Marinade Recipe

Carne asada is a staple Mexican dish, this marinade brings all the old school flavors with a spice of tex-mex into it all.

Ingredients

  • Choice of meat
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Bring your choice of meat to a bowl.
  2. Mince garlic to a paste.
  3. Add salt and pepper.
  4. Add Oil
  5. Mix
  6. Add to meat
  7. Leave in fridge for 5 hours
  8. Cook and serve!
