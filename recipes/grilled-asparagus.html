Grilled Asparagus Recipe

Grilled asparagus are a great side dish for basically any party,carne asada or even Italian dishes!

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash and rinse asparagus.
  2. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Add Oil
  4. Mix
  5. Grill and serve!
